About

Heyo, I'm Mercy Bhakta! I hope to inspire and bring positivity to people by combining two of my passions: writing and video games. Stories shape our lives, increase diversity, and lead to a greater empathy and appreciation for others. Today, I believe video games are one of the most valuable mediums for connecting with others in our increasingly tech-oriented world.

In my free time I enjoy reading, writing (especially sci-fi and fantasy!), hiking, and more recently, drawing.

I currently live in Portland, OR and attend the wonderfully challenging and zany Reed College as a computer science major.
